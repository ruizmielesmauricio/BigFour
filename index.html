Big Four Thrash Dashboard

Interactive analysis of Metallica, Megadeth, Slayer and Anthrax




roject Description This dashboard explores the musical and audience dynamics of the “Big Four” of thrash metal—Metallica, Megadeth, Slayer, and Anthrax—by combining data from multiple sources including Last.fm, MusicBrainz, and GetSongBPM. It provides an interactive environment to analyze popularity, discography trends, and musical characteristics such as tempo (BPM) across bands and albums.
